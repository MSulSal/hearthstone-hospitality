# Content Intake for Final Visual Pass

Use this checklist to finalize the front-end look quickly before CRM, restaurant ops, and gift-shop functionality are fully connected.

## What we need from the client

### 1) Brand assets
- Primary logo file (prefer transparent `PNG` or `SVG`; `WEBP` also works).
- Optional icon-only logo for small/mobile header use.
- Brand-safe minimum clear-space guidance (if they have one).

### 2) Photography (current property)
- Exterior daytime hero photos (no text overlays).
- 6 to 12 room photos across key room types.
- Courtyard / flowers / seating area photos.
- Restaurant-related photos (food/service area) that reflect current reality.
- Gift-shop/product shelf photos (if available).

### 3) Testimonials / review proof
- 3 to 6 approved guest quotes the owner is comfortable featuring.
- Source label for each quote (Tripadvisor, Google, direct guest feedback, etc.).
- Any claims that must be exact (rating, ranking, review count) and the source date.

### 4) Inn descriptions (owner-approved)
- One short homepage positioning paragraph.
- Room summary blurbs (1 to 3 sentences per room type).
- Restaurant summary (current service + near-term expansion note).
- Gift-shop summary (what is sold now + what is coming next).

### 5) Operational contact details
- Booking/inquiry phone and email.
- Front desk hours.
- Restaurant hours.
- Gift-shop hours (if separate).
