## Recurring guest themes to reinforce
- Clean, comfortable, cozy stays
- Friendly and accommodating management/staff
- Train-station convenience (across from the depot)
- Walkable access to restaurants/shops
- Courtyard/garden and outdoor sitting appeal
- Historic character with practical modern touches

## Risk themes to address in positioning
- Some food-service complaints appear in reviews
- Keep lodging trust signal strong without over-claiming dining consistency
- Avoid broad "best in class" claims without a verifiable badge on launch day

## Copy-safe trust statements (website-ready)
Use these as reusable lines in hero/support sections:

1. "Known for clean, comfortable rooms and friendly hospitality in the heart of Chama."
2. "Across from the Cumbres & Toltec depot, with downtown convenience and courtyard calm."
3. "A historic inn with welcoming service, walkable access, and a quieter pace."
4. "Guests return for comfort, location, and staff that make every stay feel easy."

## Claims we should avoid unless re-verified on launch day
- "Top 1% Best of the Best" (only use if explicit property-level badge is visible and current)
- Absolute superlatives like "best hotel in Chama"
- Spa-resort claims that imply full resort amenities if not currently offered

## Deployment checklist for review-backed copy
- Re-check rating/review count before go-live
- Replace any stale numeric claims
- Keep trust copy balanced: warmth + convenience + authenticity
- Ensure review language aligns with real on-site experience
