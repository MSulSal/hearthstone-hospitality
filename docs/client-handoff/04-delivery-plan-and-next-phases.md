# 04) Delivery Plan and Next Phases (Client-Facing)

## Phase 1: Prototype hardening (now)
Goal: make current operations prototype reliable for live stakeholder validation.

Deliver:
- tighter input consistency (including phone formatting and date hygiene)
- clearer operational alerts and queues
- vocabulary mapping support for Chama Station Inn language

## Phase 2: Inn-specific model expansion
Goal: align system semantics to Chama Station Inn workflows.

Deliver:
- room-theme and experience-oriented preference fields
- refined status transitions aligned to inn SOP
- tailored dashboards and queue priorities

## Phase 3: PWA module lift-out
Goal: move high-frequency workflows into a faster, mobile-first interface.

Deliver:
- API-backed operations screens
- touch-friendly desk/ops flows
- realtime-ready event/status architecture

## Phase 4: Production operations platform
Goal: establish a durable, supportable inn operations system.

Deliver:
- role and permission hardening
- monitoring and quality controls
- documented release/support process

## Why staged delivery is right for a 9-room inn
- avoids risky full rebuild before workflow validation
- gets immediate operational value while controlling cost
- keeps room to adapt after owner/team feedback

## Post-demo close plan
1. Lock term mapping and status naming.
2. Confirm room-theme and guest-experience data requirements.
3. Approve Phase 2 sprint scope and timeline.
4. Begin implementation with agreed acceptance checkpoints.
